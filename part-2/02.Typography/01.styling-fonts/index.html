Font Catagories

  • Serif

    • Serif fonts have small line or stroke in the end of the character. used in : Newspapers,Magazine and so on.

    1. Georgia
    2. Times New Roman etc... are in this category.

  • Sans-serif

    • San serif have no stroke or margin

    1. Avenir
    2. Arial
    3. Futura
    4. Helvetica
    5. Roboto and many more.
  • Monospace

    • MonoSpace means one space. This fonts are diferent from others and all charcter are exactly same to look.
